Adventure in Mendoza River

Navigate thrilling Class III and IV rapids on the Mendoza River, surrounded by rugged desert landscapes and stunning Andean views. Ideal for adventure lovers, this trip offers fast-paced action, expert guides, and an unforgettable rafting experience in one of Argentina’s most iconic destinations..

Adventure in Paraná River

Explore the iconic Paraná River with a thrilling rafting adventure that combines exciting rapids and calm stretches perfect for taking in the breathtaking scenery. Ideal for beginners and families, this trip offers a fun and safe introduction to white-water rafting surrounded by lush vegetation and diverse wildlife along one of South America’s most important waterways.

Adventure in Neuquén River

Experience the wild spirit of Patagonia on the Neuquén River, where crystal-clear waters and dramatic mountain landscapes set the scene for adrenaline-filled rapids. This trip is perfect for adventure seekers looking for a dynamic challenge, with technical sections, refreshing swimming spots, and spectacular views that make every moment unforgettable.
